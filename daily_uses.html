Daily Uses

Artificial Intelligence Use About More
Gaming Almost all games across the world have NPCs, or non playable characters. Many games, such as Mario, Legend of Zelda, and Elden Ring, exclusively have NPC characters you interact with. What makes these NPCs so fun is their AI. This ranges from having the NPCs attack you if you come near them, to having dialogues with you. Even board games now use AI. A great example of this is Chess, in which you can fight NPCs that are way better than the best chess player in the world.
Search Engines If you have ever used a search engine, you would notice after a few searches, you would start to get recommended things based off of what you previously wrote. This is the work of Artificial Intelligence adapting to your previous searches.
Navigation Before navigation AI, you would have to use maps to find where you have to go. However with the new navigation apps with google maps, you will be shown the fastest route that takes into account traffic, route fees, accidents, and much more.
Streaming Services Just as Search Engines adapt to your previous searches, streaming services such as Netflix, Twitch, and Youtube, adapt to your preferences, displaying them in a recommended page.
Chat Bots One of the newest ways AI is being applied is chat bots. Chat bots use AI to give you the most accurate possible response to any questions you might have regarding any topic. You could be wondering how to make pizza flavored ice cream, or how to insert chess into a website, chat bots can answer anything.
